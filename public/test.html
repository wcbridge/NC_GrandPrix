NC Grand Prix

How do you compare against the pros.

Wow I can't believe I'm that slow!

--Raleigh,NC commuter 2018

This app aims to let users compare their daily commute routes against Formula 1 drivers. As a user start by choosing from a list of your favortite drivers to compare yourself to. Next, enter your route distance and the time it took to complete said route and hit submit. Check under results to see how you compare! Future additions will let users who enjoy to race compare their own track results against Formula 1 Drivers on courses with similar distance to their own.
